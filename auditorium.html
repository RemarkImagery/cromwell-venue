Overview

Where the stage comes alive

The auditorium is the centrepiece of the Cromwell Community Venue — a flexible, purpose-built performance space designed to host everything from intimate recitals to full-scale theatrical productions, concerts, and community events.

With over 300 retractable seats, a full stage house and fly tower, state-of-the-art acoustics, and configurable flat-floor mode, the auditorium adapts to whatever the moment demands.

Concert Mode

Every seat, the best seat

In full concert configuration, the retractable tiered seating extends to provide 300+ seats with unobstructed sightlines and carefully tuned acoustics. The raked geometry ensures every audience member feels close to the performance.

Flat-Floor Mode

Flex to fit the occasion

Retract the seating to reveal a generous flat floor — perfect for exhibitions, markets, large-scale workshops, school assemblies, or standing-room concerts. The space transforms completely, offering one of the most versatile event floors in the region.
