Our Spaces

Explore the venue

Six distinct spaces designed to flex, inspire, and bring people together.

Auditorium

The heart of performance

300+ seat theatre with retractable seating, concert and flat-floor modes, full stage house and fly tower.

Cinema

Your screen in the south

Community cinema with state-of-the-art projection and sound. Independent, arthouse, and blockbuster programming.

Foyer & Cafe

Where conversations begin

Generous welcoming hub with integrated cafe, seating areas, and pre-function space flowing to the terrace.

Exhibition Hall

Stories of our place

New home for the Cromwell Museum with permanent and temporary galleries, research room, and retail space.

Function Rooms

Flex to fit

Small, medium, large, and XL configurable rooms for conferences, weddings, workshops, and community groups.

Memorial Garden & Plaza

Honour and gather

Contemplative memorial garden, entry plaza, and terraces with views to the Kawarau River and beyond.

Cafe

Good food, good company

The venue's integrated cafe sits at the heart of the foyer — a place to meet before a show, linger after an exhibition, or simply drop in for excellent coffee and locally-sourced food. Open daily with indoor and terrace seating overlooking the memorial gardens.

Cinema

Your screen in the south

Cromwell's dedicated community cinema brings world-class film to Central Otago. From new releases and arthouse favourites to special screenings and film festivals — experience cinema as it should be, in a purpose-built space with premium sound and projection.
